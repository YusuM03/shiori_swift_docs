Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

SHIORI ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal reading library app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (when signing in with email/password)
  • Name and email from Google or Apple when using social sign-in
  • Authentication tokens (managed securely by Firebase Authentication)

Content You Save

  • URLs, article titles, and metadata of items you save
  • Categories, memos, priorities, and reading status you set
  • Starred items and reading activity (timestamps of actions)

Automatically Collected

  • Article text content extracted from saved URLs (used for AI summary generation)
  • Preview images extracted from saved URLs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To create and manage your account
  • Core Functionality: To save, organize, and display your reading library
  • AI Summaries: Article text is sent to Google Gemini API to generate summaries, which are stored with your items
  • Notifications: Local notifications for daily reading reminders (scheduled on your device only, not sent to any server)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API: AI-powered article summarization. Article URLs and extracted text content are sent to Google for processing. Google AI Terms
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • FxTwitter API: Metadata retrieval for X/Twitter links (public API, no user data sent)

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or camera/microphone access
  • Device advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or tracking data (Firebase Analytics is disabled)
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) and protected by Firebase Authentication. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual items within the app at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

SHIORI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
